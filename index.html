Meet Ravi: From Hunger to Honor Student

10-year-old Ravi from Karnataka was struggling with malnutrition and irregular school attendance. Since receiving daily nutritious meals through Akshaya Patra, his attendance improved to 95% and he's now excelling in his studies.

"The meals give me energy to focus on my studies. I want to become a teacher and help other children like me." - Ravi
