About Akshaya Patra

Nourishing Bodies, Nurturing Dreams

Our Vision

"No child in India shall be deprived of education because of hunger."

We envision a future where every child has access to nutritious food and quality education, enabling them to reach their full potential and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Mission

To eliminate classroom hunger by implementing the Mid-Day Meal Scheme in government schools and government-aided schools.

We strive to fight malnutrition and support the right to education of socio-economically disadvantaged children across India.

Our Story

The Akshaya Patra Foundation was established in 2000 with the humble mission of feeding 1,500 children in 5 schools in Bengaluru. What started as a small initiative by ISKCON Bengaluru has now grown into the world's largest NGO-run mid-day meal program.

Our journey began when we realized that many children in India were not attending school regularly due to hunger. We understood that providing nutritious meals could be a powerful incentive for children to come to school and stay enrolled.

Over the past two decades, we have expanded our reach across 14 states and 2 union territories, serving freshly cooked, nutritious meals to over 2.1 million children daily. Our state-of-the-art centralized kitchens use cutting-edge technology while maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and food safety.
